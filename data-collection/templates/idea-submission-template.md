## Tips for Better Validation Results

✅ **Be specific**: "Middle-class students in tier 2 cities struggling with IIT prep" is better than "students"  
✅ **Justify numbers**: Always explain how you calculated TAM/SAM/SOM  
✅ **Be honest**: Acknowledge risks and competition; don't overclaim  
✅ **Focus on your unfair advantage**: What do YOU have that competitors don't?  
✅ **Show traction**: If you have users/revenue, share it (it strengthens your case)
